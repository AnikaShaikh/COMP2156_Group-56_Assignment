DEVOPS 2156, a course offered at George Brown College in Toronto, Ontario, delves into the principles and practices of DevOps,
which entails seamlessly integrating development and operations throughout the software development lifecycle. The curriculum
encompasses various subjects such as continuous integration, continuous delivery, infrastructure as code, automation, monitoring,
and collaborative tools. Through this course, students acquire skills to streamline the software development process, increase
deployment frequency, and elevate product quality by implementing DevOps methodologies and utilizing relevant tools. Hands-on
projects and assignments are integral components of the course, reinforcing learning outcomes and equipping students for real-world
scenarios in the realms of software development and IT operations.
